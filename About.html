About Me



I romanticize every part of my life — especially the scents I wear. This little archive is part memory box, part scent journal. It started as a way to keep track of the fragrances I wore on certain days — the ones that made me feel delicate, dangerous, or divine.

Now, it's become a space to explore how scent shapes emotion, identity, and even fantasy. You'll find everything from old favorites to new obsessions, dreamy notes, layered pairings, and the feelings each one unlocks. Welcome to my library of bottled memories.
